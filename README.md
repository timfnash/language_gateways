# Language Gateways

*How the languages you don't speak reveal what you're missing in those you do.*

---

## What Is This?

Language Gateways is a speaker/trainer brand built by [Tim F Nash](https://www.linkedin.com/in/timfnash/) on a simple thesis:

**Language is primarily a tool for thought, not communication.** In an age where AI translation is free and instant, monolingual thinking has become a strategic liability. The competitive advantage belongs to people who can think from multiple perspectives—the ones who understand that each language is a gateway to a different way of seeing the world.

This repo holds the **branding assets** and **social media builder** for Language Gateways.

## Using the Social Image Builder

### Quick Start

1. **Open:** https://timfnash.github.io/language_gateways/language-gateways-builder.html
2. **Select template type** from dropdown (Quote, Stat, Concept, or Image)
3. **Enter your content** (text, number, or upload image)
4. **Adjust font sizes** with sliders if needed (defaults are pre-set and centered)
5. **Download PNG** (1080×1080px, ready to post)

### Template Guide

#### Quote
Use for:
- Author quotes
- Philosophy or insight statements
- Key thesis restatements

**Example:**
> "If you don't know other languages you don't know your own."  
> GOETHE

#### Statistic
Use for:
- Impact numbers (e.g., "7,000 languages in the world")
- Surprising facts or research findings
- Any stat + supporting context

**Example:**
> **48**  
> Languages spoken in one Jersey primary school.  
> That's not a problem. That's your competitive advantage.

#### Concept
Use for:
- Core insights or questions
- Headlines + supporting line
- Purple text on light background (high contrast)

**Example:**
> The Rose: Same flower, twelve different ways of seeing it.  
> What perspectives are you missing?

#### Image
Use for:
- Photos with optional text overlay
- Hero images with captions
- Visual storytelling

Upload your photo → add text (optional) → choose text colour (white/purple/magenta/gold) → download.

## Core Thesis in 60 Seconds

**The Communication Problem Is Solved**  
AI translation is free and instant. If language study is *only* about exchanging information, it's redundant.

**The Thinking Problem Remains**  
Every language encodes a different way of seeing the world:
- Turkish sees a rose as "the quintessential flower" (*gül*)
- Hindi sees it as the "flower of water" (*gulab*)
- Somali sees it as "rising from the ground" (*kacay*)

You may never speak Turkish or Hindi. But those languages reveal perspectives on a rose—and on your world—that you'd otherwise miss.

**The Opportunity**  
As organisations face complex, interdependent challenges, monolingual thinking is a liability. The ability to see situations from multiple linguistic/cognitive perspectives is a strategic asset.

Language Gateways helps leaders, teams, and educators recover this asset—not by forcing people to learn new languages, but by helping them think differently using the languages already in their midst.
